Welcome to the official website of the Democratic Commonwealth of Locibarpulo!

What is Locibarpulo?

Locibarpulo is a micronation (link) in North America. Locibarpulo was founded by Mel Stormcloak, the first Prime Minister, in 2024. The core ideals of the nation are equality, liberty, and environmental preservation.

How Can I Join?

You can join Locibarpulo through the official Revolt server (link).

A Member State of the UPM

Locibarpulo is a member state of the Union of Progressive Micronations (link). The UPM is a micronational alliance simmilar to the EU, in that it has a single centralized currency for all member states ($UPMD), open borders, a centralized ID & passport system, and a constitution that all member states must adhere to.
